About Bulk Notifications

Bulk notifications allow you to send the same message to multiple applicants simultaneously.

Use Cases:
  • Announce company events
  • Share important updates
  • Remind applicants about deadlines
  • Notify about position closures
Target Options:
  • All Applicants: Every registered applicant
  • Job Applicants: Only those who applied for a specific job
  • Status Group: Applicants with a specific application status
