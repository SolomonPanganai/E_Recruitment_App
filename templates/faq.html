Frequently Asked Questions

Click the "Register" button in the top navigation bar. Fill in your personal details including your name, email, and create a password. Once registered, you can log in and start applying for positions.

Yes, you can apply for as many positions as you qualify for. However, you can only submit one application per position. Make sure to tailor your cover letter for each application.

Required:
  • Updated CV/Resume (PDF, DOC, or DOCX format)
Recommended:
  • Certified copy of ID document
  • Certified copies of qualifications
  • Cover letter explaining your suitability
Maximum file size is 16MB per document.

Log in to your account and go to "My Dashboard". You'll see all your applications with their current status. Possible statuses include:
  • Submitted: Application received
  • Under Review: Being evaluated by HR
  • Shortlisted: Selected for further consideration
  • Interviewed: Interview completed
  • Offered: Job offer extended
  • Unsuccessful: Not selected

Yes, you can withdraw your application at any time before receiving a final decision. Go to your application details page and click "Withdraw Application". Note that this action cannot be undone.

Employment Equity positions are designated to promote equal opportunity and fair treatment in employment. Some positions may indicate an EE preference. All applicants are encouraged to apply, and the EE designation helps us meet our transformation goals.

You will receive email notifications at each stage of the process. Please ensure your email address is correct and check your spam folder regularly. You can also log in to check your application status at any time.

Contact the HR department via the contact page or email hr@municipality.gov.za with your registered email address. We will assist you in resetting your password after verifying your identity.
